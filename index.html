Bangkok

Bangkok, the capital of Thailand, is a vibrant city known for its bustling street life, ornate temples, and rich cultural heritage. It is a major hub for tourism, shopping, and nightlife. Visitors are drawn to its unique blend of tradition and modernity, with towering skyscrapers alongside ancient structures.

Estambul

Istanbul, Turkey's largest city, uniquely spans two continents—Europe and Asia—divided by the Bosphorus Strait. With a history spanning over 8,500 years, it has served as the capital of the Roman, Byzantine, and Ottoman Empires. Today, Istanbul is a vibrant metropolis, blending rich historical heritage with modern cultural and economic significance.

Londres

London, the capital of the United Kingdom, is a global city known for its history, culture, and landmarks such as the Tower of London, Buckingham Palace, and the British Museum. It is a hub for finance, arts, and education. The city's diverse population and blend of tradition and innovation make it a dynamic place to visit and live.

More info

Hong Kong

Hong Kong is a vibrant global city known for its impressive skyline, bustling harbor, and unique blend of Eastern and Western cultures. It is a major financial and business hub, attracting people from all over the world. The city offers a rich mix of modern architecture, historical sites, and delicious cuisine.

More info

Dubái

Dubai, located in the United Arab Emirates, is a modern city famous for its futuristic skyline, luxury shopping, and towering landmarks like the Burj Khalifa. It is a global hub for business, tourism, and innovation. The city combines traditional Arabian culture with cutting-edge architecture and entertainment.

More info

París

Paris, the capital of France, is renowned for its art, fashion, and iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum. The city is a global center for culture, cuisine, and history. Its charming streets, cafes, and romantic ambiance attract millions of visitors each year.

Best Restaurants Ranking 2024

1

Disfrutar

Having worked together at El Bulli, one of the world's most influential restaurants, Oriol Castro, Mateu Casañas, and Eduard Xatruch have since achieved remarkable success with their own ventures. After opening Compartir in Cadaqués, they launched Disfrutar in 2014, solidifying their reputation as culinary innovators.

2

Asador Etxebarri

Nestled in the lush foothills of the Spanish Basque Country, Asador Etxebarri has occupied the same rustic stone building since the 1990s. With sweeping views of the mountains and grazing livestock from its terrace (where welcome snacks are served), the restaurant boasts a deep connection to history and nature.

3

Bruno Verjus

One of the novelties to have won the award for the highest entry in the list. Bruno Verjus, a 63-year-old chef, opened his restaurant at 54 after having lived many lives, having been a medical student, a businessman in China, a photographer, an author, a radio commentator, and even a food critic.

4

DiverXO

DiverXO, the brainchild of Dabiz Muñoz, renowned for his daring approach to cuisine and widely regarded as one of the world's most creative chefs, has slipped from third to fourth place this year. However, with its three Michelin stars, it remains the top-rated restaurant in Madrid. A truly sensory experience..
